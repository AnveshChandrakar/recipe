Recipe

Chocolate Cake

"Here is a fast and easy version of the classic Chocolate Cake Recipe.Enjoy!"

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total Time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingridients

Black Chocolate: 100g

Sugar: 150g

Butter: 200g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80g

Instructions

1

Melt the chocolate and the butter together

2

Mix the Sugar,Eggs and the flour

3

Mix Everything to get a smooth dough

4

Bake in the oven
